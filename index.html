Protein Pancake

Healthy and delicious chocolate-banana protein pancakes to start your day with energy!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs and protein powder, then mix well.
  3. Heat a pan and pour small amounts of the batter.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.
